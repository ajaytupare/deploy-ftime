A car accident is an unexpected event in which one or more vehicles collide with another vehicle, object, or person, often causing injuries, damage, or loss of life.
Common causes include speeding, distracted driving, reckless behavior, poor weather conditions, and driving under the influence of alcohol or drugs.
Car accidents can have serious physical, emotional, and financial consequences for those involved.
Following traffic rules, wearing seat belts, and staying attentive while driving can help reduce the risk of accidents and improve road safety.
